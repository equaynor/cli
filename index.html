Back to the roots

Kemetic yoga or Smai Tawi is an ancient Egyptian yoga practice. Smai Tawi literally means union of the two lands referring to upper and lower Egypt representing the higher and lower aspects of self.

Through the process of the Kemetic Yoga system of postures we are able to refine our power of creation and achieve the great awakening of consciousness.
